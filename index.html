About Us

Who are we?

We are a team of engineering students from the University of Kansas that are passionate about teaching middle school students to coding.

What is our mission?

We believe that coding is a fundamental skill that improves our problem-solving, diversifies our thinking, and provides a foundation for understanding a technologically-advanced world.

What do we teach?

We teach students how to use coding as a problem-solving tool. Our curriculum focuses on the fundamentals of Python and can be customized and paced to meet the needs of your students.
